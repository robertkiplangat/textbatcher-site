What it does

TextBatcher helps you break long text into smaller chunks that fit platform limits. Useful for messaging apps, social media, and structured posting.

How it works

  1. Paste your text
  2. Choose your limit
  3. Copy each batch instantly

How to Split Long Text Without Losing Meaning

Splitting long text manually often breaks sentences and disrupts flow. A better approach is to split at natural points such as full stops or spaces, ensuring each section remains readable and complete.

Tools like TextBatcher automate this process, helping you maintain structure while working within character limits.
