Character Limits for Popular Platforms

A simple guide to help you stay within limits and avoid broken text.

Most online platforms have character limits that restrict how much text you can post at once. If you exceed these limits, your content may be cut off or rejected.

Why character limits exist

Character limits help platforms keep content concise and readable. They also improve performance and user experience.

Common character limits

Twitter (X)

280 characters per tweet.

WhatsApp

Large messages are allowed, but long text becomes difficult to read and manage.

Instagram

Captions can be up to 2,200 characters, but shorter content performs better.

Facebook

Posts can be very long, but readability drops with large text blocks.

Problems caused by character limits

  • Text gets cut off unexpectedly
  • Messages become difficult to structure
  • Users are forced to split content manually

How to handle long text properly

The best way to deal with character limits is to split your text into smaller, structured sections. Each section should remain readable and complete.
