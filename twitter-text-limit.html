What is the Twitter character limit?

Most tweets are limited to 280 characters. If your message exceeds this, you’ll need to split it into multiple tweets or a thread.

Why splitting text manually is a problem

  • It breaks sentences awkwardly
  • It takes time to count characters
  • You can lose context between parts

The better way to split text

The best way to split long text is to break it at natural points, such as spaces or punctuation. This keeps each part readable and complete.
